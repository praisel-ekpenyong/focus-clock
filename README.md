# Focus Clock

**All your time tools in one place.**

Focus Clock is a fast, private productivity web app built for people who want to plan their day, track focus sessions, and keep time across the world — without switching between apps or creating an account.

---

## Why Focus Clock

- **One app, six tools** — everything you need to manage time and tasks
- **Private by default** — your data stays in your browser, not on a server
- **Works anywhere** — desktop, mobile, and installable as a PWA
- **Always ready** — open the app and start in seconds

---

## Features

### Pomodoro
Stay in flow with customizable focus and break timers. Attach tasks to sessions, track daily completions, reorder your to-do list, and use picture-in-picture or fullscreen when you need to zone in.

### Timezones
Compare cities side by side on a live grid. Scrub through hours to plan meetings across zones, switch between 12 and 24-hour formats, and share your layout with a link.

### Daily Planner
See yesterday, today, and tomorrow at a glance. Add tasks per day, set up recurring routines, and capture quick ideas in a task dump until you're ready to schedule them.

### World Clock
Keep a personal list of cities with a large, readable clock for your selected location. Add or remove cities in seconds.

### Timer
Run countdowns with built-in presets or your own custom durations. Get interval chimes during long sessions and a clear alarm when time is up.

### Stopwatch
Measure elapsed time with millisecond precision. Record laps and track performance without losing your place.

### Built for focus
- Ambient focus sounds — rain, white noise, and forest
- Configurable alarm sounds when sessions end
- Edmonton and Toronto included by default; 40+ cities searchable worldwide

---

## Built for production

Focus Clock is a lightweight, static web application — no login, no backend, no install required. Use it in the browser or add it to your home screen for an app-like experience with offline support.
